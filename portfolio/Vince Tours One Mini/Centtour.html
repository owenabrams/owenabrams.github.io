Central Tour (5 Days)

Source of the River Nile & Murchinson Falls

Day 1: Arrival, transferred to Hotel, Afternoon have an optional visit to the Uganda wildlife Education Centre for an informative guided walk depending on the time of arrival. O/N Serena Lake Victoria Hotel/Imperial Botanical Beach Hotel/Central Inn HB Basis.
Day 2:Drive to Jinja upon the way experience the tea, sugar-cane plantations and Natural forests of Mabira then Source of the Nile and other waterfalls later on retire at the hotel in the evening. O/N Serena Lake Victoria Hotel/Imperial Botanical Beach Hotel/Central Inn HB Basis.


Day 3:After breakfast, drive west of Uganda to Murchison Falls National Park with a stopover at ziwa rhino sanctuary. In the evening, visit top of the falls. O/N Chobe/ Para Safari Lodge /Red chili HB Basis.

Day 4:Early morning game drive, return to the lodge for breakfast and lunch. Afternoon boat cruise upon the Nile launch trip with refreshing diversity of animal and bird species. Return to the lodge late in the evening. O/N Chobe/ Para Safari Lodge /Red chili HB Basis.

Day 5:After early morning breakfast, drive to Entebbe arriving in the afternoon. Visit Kabazaire project, an orphanage that the company supports then fly out.

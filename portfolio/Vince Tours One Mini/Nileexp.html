Nile Experience Safari (6 Days)

Source of the River Nile & Murchinson Falls

This 6 day, safari to Uganda, will take you through the journey of when the Nile starts it journey have fun on it, look at its wonder in Murchison. To the East to visit the source of the Nile, Ssezibwa Cultural site, adventure rafting on the Nile before heading towards the North to visit Uganda’s largest conservation area: Murchison Falls National Park famous for Murchison Falls, and a variety of wild game. You will have what to talk about back home.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01:Arrival at the Airport, transfer to Kampala The city of Impalas built on seven hills originally. Kampala is Uganda’s Capital City. Dinner and Overnight. HB BasisO/N Humura Resort on HB Basis.

Day 02: After breakfast, you will set off for whole day exploration at Jinja. This tour will take you to where the mighty Nile starts its long journey to Sudan, Egypt and the Mediterranean Sea. Enroute, you will branch off to Ssezibwa falls observations in vegetation, relief that are worth seeing. You will head east passing through coffee, Tea and Sugar plantations and the colorful roadside fruit/vegetable markets not forgetting the drive through Mabira forest. Continue to Jinja to view the source of the Nile. This is a spot where John Speke first discovered the source of the Nile River. Boat cruise is a must to the actual source where the Lake water and the river water separate. Lunch will be in Jinja, you may opt for a short relaxation in town for shopping in one of the towns still with the colonial buildings. Retire to the hotel arriving late in the eveningO/N Jinja Nile Resort on FB Basis.

Day 03: Wake up in the morning to a Heavy breakfast, transfer to briefing point for the start of rafting. This will take a full day of safe fun, adventure all day. Return late in the evening to Kampala.O/N Humura Resort on FB Basis.

Day 4:Early in the morning, you will transfer to Murchison falls National Park. Drive south west through Nakasongola, drive to Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary to track the white rhinos and lunch, Drive further towards MFNP, on reaching the park you will drive to the top of the falls to see how the calm Nile forces itself through a narrow gorge through which it explodes into the falls. After you will transfer to your Lodge.O/N Para Safari Lodge on HB Basis.

Day 5:Our morning safari of Murchison National park starts around 6:30am, with an early morning breakfast or packed breakfast. Drive to the Ferry crossing area to the northern bank for a thrilling game drive. Expect to see some of the big 5 (Elephant, Buffalo, Lion, Leopard) and other wildlife animals not forgetting the Giraffe; you will also have a panoramic view of Lake Albert. Later, return for breakfast and relax at the Lodge waiting for lunch. After lunch. In the afternoon, take a boat cruise to the bottom of the mighty falls; you will see Hippos, crocodiles, elephants and other animals, as well as a variety of bird species.O/N Para Lodge on FB Basis.

Day 6:After breakfast, drive through the cool tropical rain forest of Budongo on your way to Entebbe. Expect to see, road site markets for fresh fruits interacting with the locals. Continue to Entebbe through Kampala arriving late in the afternoon. Transfer to the hotel or Airport. End of Tour.

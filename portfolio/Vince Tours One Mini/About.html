About Us

GREY HORIZONS and Travel Ltd Uganda started 2007 and fully registered as a limited company in 2010, dealing in all East African safaris. We are located in Kampala at Teachers’ House building Room 208, on Bombo Road.
The company has 15 vehicles which are under surveillance (are tracked) and fully insured. We boast of a well trained professional staff with in-depth knowledge on each specific region, and take a uniquely personal pleasure in welcoming providing you with warmth, care and genuine friendship.


With GREY HORIZONS you have our personal and professional assurance that your expectations will not only be met – but far exceeded as regards your request.

Our Team
As one of the leading Fast growing Tour Company in Uganda safari destination management companies, we have a team of dedicated travel professionals, each of whom brings a wealth of practical experience and specialist knowledge to the planning of our clients' needs.
